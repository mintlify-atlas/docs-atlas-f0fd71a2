The **Dashboard** (Panel de Control) is the first screen you see after logging in. It gives you a real-time snapshot of your store's health and lets you jump to any module in one tap.

## What the Dashboard Shows

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Today's Sales" icon="shopping-cart">
    Number of individual sale transactions recorded for the current day.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Units Sold" icon="boxes-stacked">
    Total quantity of items sold across all transactions today.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Total Products" icon="warehouse">
    Count of all products currently registered in your inventory.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Last Sale Amount" icon="money-bill">
    The total value of the most recent sale, along with its ID and timestamp.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

All four metric cards update automatically as you record new sales — no manual refresh needed.

## Weekly Activity Chart

Below the metrics, the **Actividad Semanal** bar chart plots total sales revenue for each of the past 7 days (today plus the previous 6). 

- The horizontal axis shows abbreviated day names in Spanish (Lun, Mar, Mié…).
- The vertical axis displays amounts in Soles (S/).
- The chart is rendered with the [Vico](https://github.com/patrykandpatryk/vico) library using a column chart layout.
- Days with no sales show a zero-height bar, so you always see the full week at a glance.

## Low-Stock Alert Banner

If any product has fewer than **10 units** in stock, a warning banner appears automatically between the chart and the quick-access section. The banner lists up to 3 of the most critical items (sorted by lowest stock first), each showing its name and remaining quantity.

Tap **Ver todos** (or **Ver** on small screens) to navigate directly to the Products screen filtered to your full inventory.

<Warning>
  The low-stock banner only disappears when all products have 10 or more units. Restock items through the Purchases module to clear the alert.
</Warning>

## Quick-Access Shortcuts

The **Acceso Rápido** section at the bottom contains four cards that navigate directly to the app's main workflows:

| Card | Label | Destination |
|---|---|---|
| Productos | Gestionar | Products list |
| Ventas | Registrar | Sales screen |
| Compras | Registrar | Purchases screen |
| Cierre de Caja | Ver | Cash Closure screen |

Tap any card to go directly to that module.

## Navigating with the Drawer

Every screen in Bodequita, including the Dashboard, has a hamburger menu icon (☰) in the top-left corner. Tapping it opens the **navigation drawer** which lists all six sections of the app:

| Menu Item | Route | Icon |
|---|---|---|
| Panel | `dashboard` | Dashboard |
| Productos | `products` | Inventory |
| Ventas | `sales` | Shopping Cart |
| Compras | `purchases` | Add Shopping Cart |
| Cierre | `cash_closure` | Account Balance |
| Perfil | `profile` | Person |

The currently active screen is highlighted in the drawer. Tapping any item navigates directly to that section and closes the drawer automatically.

<Tip>
  You can also reach Products, Sales, Purchases, and Cash Closure directly from the Quick-Access cards without opening the drawer.
</Tip>
