title: Sales
description: Record sales transactions, manage a shopping cart with multiple products, and track daily sales history with automatic stock decrement.

The **Sales** screen (Registrar Venta) lets you build a cart of products and complete a sale in seconds. Stock levels are decremented automatically when the sale is confirmed.

## Recording a Sale

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Sales screen">
    Navigate to **Ventas** from the drawer or the Dashboard quick-access card. The screen opens with an empty cart.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select a product">
    In the **Agregar Productos** section, use the dropdown to find a product:
    - Tap the **Producto** field to open the dropdown.
    - Type part of the product name or code to filter the list (up to 12 results shown).
    - Tap a product to select it — its code is automatically filled into the **Código** field.

    Alternatively, type the product code directly into the **Código** field if you know it.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set the quantity">
    Enter the desired quantity in the **Cantidad** field. It defaults to `1`.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add to cart">
    Tap **Agregar** (the button with a + icon). The product appears in the **Productos en la Venta** section.

    - If the same product is added again, its quantity is incremented rather than creating a duplicate line.
    - If the code is not found or the quantity is invalid, a snackbar message explains the problem.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review the cart">
    The cart list shows each line item with:
    - Product name
    - Quantity × unit price
    - Line subtotal in Soles (S/)
    - A remove button (red trash icon)

    The **Resumen de Venta** panel on the right (or below on narrow screens) shows:
    - Number of distinct products
    - Total units
    - Subtotal (before tax)
    - IGV at 18%
    - **Total** (the final amount charged)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete the sale">
    Tap **Registrar Venta** (the green button with a checkmark). The sale is saved and:
    - Stock for each sold product is decremented by the sold quantity.
    - The cart is cleared automatically.
    - A snackbar confirms the sale with its assigned ID (e.g., *"Venta realizada (#5)"*).

    Tap **Cancelar** at any point to clear the entire cart without saving.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  On tablets and wide screens, the cart and summary panels are displayed side-by-side. On phones, they stack vertically and the screen is scrollable.
</Note>

## Sale Data Model

Each completed sale is stored across three related structures:

<CardGroup cols={3}>
  <Card title="Sale" icon="receipt">
    Header record with auto-generated `id`, a `fecha` timestamp (milliseconds), and the `total` amount.
  </Card>
  <Card title="SaleDetail" icon="list">
    One row per product in the sale, storing `sale_id`, `product_id`, `cantidad` (quantity), `precio_unit` (unit price at time of sale), and `subtotal`.
  </Card>
  <Card title="SaleWithDetails" icon="table">
    A combined view that embeds the `Sale` header with its full list of `SaleDetail` items — used for history and reporting.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

Additionally, `DailySaleSummary` aggregates total revenue per day (used by the Dashboard weekly chart).

## Stock Validation

The app validates stock before adding a product to the cart:

- If the product code is blank, the sale is blocked with the message *"Ingresa un código o selecciona un producto"*.
- If the quantity is zero or negative, the message *"Cantidad inválida"* is shown.
- Products with zero available stock can still be added to the cart during this version — ensure you monitor the Dashboard's low-stock banner to avoid overselling.

<Tip>
  Use the Dashboard's low-stock banner as a proactive alert. When stock drops below 10 units, the banner appears automatically so you can restock via a purchase before running out.
</Tip>

## Sales Summary in the Sale Screen

| Field | Description |
|---|---|
| Productos | Number of distinct product lines in the cart |
| Unidades | Total quantity of all items combined |
| Subtotal | Sum of all line subtotals (excluding tax) |
| IGV (18%) | Peruvian tax calculated at 18% of subtotal |
| Total | Final amount = Subtotal + IGV |
